Trump Your Goals

What is Trump Your Goals?

TrumpYourGoals.com is a free goal-setting platform that boosts your incentive to succeed by allowing you to commit to real-world stakes to hold yourself accountable. By creating a commitment contract and setting a deadline, you give yourself additional motivation to achieve.

By having to pay an Anti-Charity (an organization you oppose) in the event that you quit, you’re giving yourself some serious motivation to succeed. A Chapman University Research Study proves that you are 3x more likely to accomplish a goal if you put money on the line!

How does this work?

It's dead simple! Fill out the goal form at the top of the page, and we'll send an e-mail confirming your commitment. Just reply to the e-mail when you've completed your goal — it's that easy! Your card will only charged if you fail your goal.

*Don't worry, we'll remind you before your due date in case you forget

Are you serious about success?

If you’re really serious about success, put your money where your mouth is! Think about it. If smoking that cigarette means you have to send cold hard cash to your least favorite politician, you probably won't be reaching for that pack!
